thomasTrenholme/HighSeas

High Seas was a Minecraft 1.7.10 server and modpack that existed from 2018-2020 ran by myself and my partner Severin Field. The server, and community that grew around the server amassed around 1000 active players and achieved over 50,000 unique downloads during its peak. Hosted on the Technic Modpack platform, High Seas stayed on the platform's most trending modpacks for months at a time and quickly grew into one of the platform's busiest destinations. High Seas was also an official partner of VoidHost, a server hosting platform. Offering in-game donations, High Seas became profitable and Mr. Field and I stepped into the role of managing our active playerbase.

High Seas grew into a popular modpack and community of players that shared a bond. During 2020, Mr. Field and I became busier with additional college and life responsibilities and had to close down the server and modpack.
